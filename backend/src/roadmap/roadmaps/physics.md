# AQA A-Level Physics — Roadmap

## Overview

- **Sections 1–8** are compulsory — everyone studies these
- **Sections A–E** are optional — your school picks one for you
- **12 Required Practicals (RPs)** are spread across the compulsory content — you'll be assessed on these in your exams
- Each row below has its own lesson you can work through
